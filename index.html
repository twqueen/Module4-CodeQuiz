Coding Quiz

A test of your basic coding knowledge

This quiz follows a multiple choice format. You will be given 75 seconds to finish the quiz. For every question answered incorrectly, you will be penalized and the timer will decrease by 15 seconds. The quiz will end when either all questions have been answered or when timer runs out. A score will be recorded based on time remaining. Feel free to compete with your friends to see who can get the highest score. The top 10 scores will be recorded in Leaderboard.
